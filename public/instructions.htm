Image Ranking

In the next pages you will be shown a series of 40 images.

The images have been created by human subjects participating in a scientific experiment. For the correctness of scientific research, please try to be as much accurate as possible.

Rank each image by moving a slider on a scale from 0 (lowest grade) to 10 (highest grade) on four dimensions:
  • Creativity
  • Interestingness as a face
  • Abstractness
  • Overall appealing or quality

How the images have been created

Each image has been created in less than 60 seconds by human subjects using a special computer interface.

This interface allowed them to draw images quickly, but imposed limitations on the images that could be created.

Get a better idea of the possibilities offered by the tool by looking at a random sample of images in the next page.
