Image Ranking

In the next pages you will be shown a series of 40 images.

The images have been created by human subjects participating in a scientific experiment. For the correctness of scientific research, please try to be as much accurate as possible.

You will be asked to rank each image by moving a slider on a scale from 0 (lowest grade) to 10 (highest grade) on four dimensions:
  • Creativity
  • Interestingness as a face
  • Abstractness
  • Overall appealing or quality
In your evaluation, do not consider the color of the lines, but just the shapes and figures they form.

How the images have been created

Each image has been created in 60 seconds time by human subjects using a semi-automated, parametric tool.

This tool allows to draw images quickly, but imposes limitations in the types of shapes that is possible to draw.

You will get a better idea of the possibilities offered by the tool by observing a random sample of images in the next page.
