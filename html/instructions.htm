Image categorization

In the next pages you will be shown a series of 50-60 images.

The images have been created by human subjects participating in a scientific experiment. For the correctness of scientific research, please try to be as much accurate as possible.

You will be asked to express a quality judgement on each image by moving a slider on a scale from 0 to 10, where:
  • 0 (zero) means complete dislike
  • 5 (five) means that you are indifferent
  • 10 (ten) means complete like

Parametric images

What the subject could draw was limited by the parametric tool used to generate the images. You will get a better idea of the possibilities offered by the tool by observing a random sample of pictures in the next page. Please take into account the limitations of the tool when expressing your judgement.
