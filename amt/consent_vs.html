Consent

The data for the experiment you participated in can be used in a research study to understand how groups collaborate.

  • You must be 18 to participate.
  • Your participation is voluntary and uncompensated.
  • You can stop contributing data at any time.
  • Your data will remain private and confidential.
  • Only data related to your performance will be used.
  • The possible risks of this research are minimal.

This experiment has been approved by Northeastern University (IRB #13-03-09). This research is funded, in part, by the US Office of Naval Research and by the Army Research Office. The research sponsor's human subject protection personnel will have access to the study records and data. If you have questions about this experiment, contact irb@volunteerscience.com. If you have questions about your rights as a participant, contact irb@neu.edu.
